My D&D Google Drive

My Google drive is full of almost all of the official D&D books in PDF form and character sheet templates (many with auto-math macros).

This is also where I host all my campaigns. So look here for session 0 information, art (maps, drawings, pictures, etc.), your party members' character sheets, and the spreadsheet where I keep every character's current status (conditions & HP)!

Stenograph

Stenograph is an open-source web app that simulates a D&D character sheet. Click the link above to open it in a new tab!

Don't forget to save your character once you've made it! Stenograph will save your character data as a JSON. Hold on to this file and then simply click load and upload the JSON to view it again later.

Player's General Rules

The Player's General Rules is written by me with the intention of putting all the most commonly referenced D&D rules and my favorite variant rules in one place. Unless otherwise stated or specific to the campaign, these are the rules I defer to when I DM.

I recommend you download it and use the header/bookmark feature of PDFs. Otherwise it can be cumbersome to navigate.

The only topic I do not cover in this document is character creation. Other than using the documents in my Google Drive, keep scrolling on this webpage for more information on character creation!

SubclassGuide.com

A web app that allows you to view all official Races(and their Subraces) as well as Backgrounds. You can then toggle each source book so you can only view from a subset of the total.
